# 🌱 Investment Garden

Investment Garden is a cozy web-based financial literacy simulation game designed for students from Class 6 to Class 10. Instead of teaching finance through traditional theory, the game introduces Equity Basics through interaction, emotional learning, and gameplay-driven experiences.

Players manage a magical garden where every plant represents a different type of investment. Through weather events, plant growth, risk management, and long-term decision-making, students naturally learn concepts like ownership, diversification, volatility, patience, and compounding.

---

# 🎮 Core Idea

The game transforms investing concepts into a living ecosystem.

- 🌵 Stable plants represent safer investments
- 🍄 Risky plants represent high-risk, high-reward assets
- 🌪️ Storms represent market crashes
- ☀️ Growth seasons represent bull markets
- 🌳 Long-term trees teach patience and compounding

Instead of memorizing financial definitions, players learn by experiencing consequences and making decisions inside the simulation.

---

# 📚 Educational Goals

Investment Garden is designed to teach:

- Equity Basics
- Ownership & Shares
- Risk vs Reward
- Diversification
- Long-Term Investing
- Market Volatility
- Emotional Decision Making
- Compound Growth

The experience is intentionally visual, emotional, and beginner-friendly for younger students.
